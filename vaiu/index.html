Raiffeisen Bank Kosova

What can I do with this new service?

You can withdraw cash from your bank account anytime, anywhere without the need to go to a physical cash machine.

What can I do with my cash?

You can give that cash to anyone you choose.

What can that person do with the cash?

That person can give the cash to someone else or claim the cash and deposit it into their bank account

Who has access to this service to withdraw cash?

This service will be available to our customers who have had an account in good standing for at least 3 months and who maintain at least €100 in their current amount.

How do I start using this service?

You will receive an invitation to activate this service. You're invitation will be sent to your mobile phone number as registered with us. An email invitation will also be sent to your email address registered with us. Please update your phone and email address to be sure to receive this invitation.

Is there a charge for withdrawing cash?

Withdrawals will cost 0.20€. However, customers who activate their account for this service in the first 3 months after receiving their invitation, will be able to withdraw up to a total of €1000 each month without any fees for 12 months.

Is there a fee to deposit cash I receive from someone?

Well, that depends. The following fees will apply:

  • For cash that was withdrawn more than 1 hour ago, there will be no fee to deposit that cash.
  • In other cases, depositing cash to your account will cost 20cents.

Can I use my cash to buy things in stores?

Yes. Any store can choose to accept your cash. Raiffeisen business customers are automatically enabled for receiving your payments. Other store owners, please visit a Raiffeisen branch near you to open a business account to save up to thousands per year on payment processing fees.

Are there any other advantages to using this service instead of getting paper cash from an ATM?

Many. Too many to list here, in fact. We're confident that once you start to use digital cash, you will think of the current ATM as you think of the old payphone cabins before you got your mobile phone. For the younger generation out there, we older ones used to have to walk to a special cabin with a phone inside and pay to use the phone for a few minutes.

How can I keep track of my withdrawals?

Your Raiffeisen digital ATM will show you each withdrawal with its amount and the time of withdrawal.

Can I redeposit my cash myself?

No. However, if your cash is not deposited by someone else after 3 days, it will be redeposited automatically to your current account. There is a small fee of 5cents for unused cash withdrawals.

How will cash withdrawals appear on my bank statement?

Your deposits and withdrawals will appear just as they do now. Instead of the address of the ATM you used, you will see "Vaiu Digital ATM" as the source of the withdrawal or deposit. Any associated fees will also be on your statement.
